What is Sports Card Break?

In sports card breaks, individuals or businesses buy entire boxes of sports cards and then offer collectors the chance to "buy in" and claim cards from specific teams within the box. The "breaker," who purchased the box, opens it via live stream to reveal the cards inside. Participants who bought a specific team get to keep the cards from that team. For instance, if a participant purchased the Los Angeles Lakers and there are two Lakers cards in the box, they get to keep those cards. However, participants who bought teams without any cards receive nothing from the break.
